I work on natural language processing and machine learning, with a primary focus on reasoning. My work explores reasoning in two directions:

  • Modeling: I develop techniques that can accurately reason over real-world scenarios. Methodologically, I combine neural models with principled methods to train strong reasoning models without reliance on human supervision.
  • Evaluation: I create benchmarks that reliably reflect the performance of LMs when deployed in the real world. To achieve this, I draw inspiration from user-chatbot interactions observed in natural environments and focus on addressing real-world problems that already exist.
I am extremely fortunate to be advised by Claire Cardie and Sasha Rush.
